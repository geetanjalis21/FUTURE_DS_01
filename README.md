# 📊 Business Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to identify key trends, top-performing products, and regional performance. The objective is to generate actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard Creation)

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Sales Trend Analysis
- Visualized sales trends over time using line charts
- Identified growth patterns and seasonal fluctuations

### 2. Sales by Category
- Compared revenue across different product categories
- Highlighted top-performing categories

### 3. Top Products
- Identified top-selling products based on total sales
- Ranked products to understand contribution to revenue

### 4. Sales by Region
- Analyzed regional performance
- Identified high and low performing regions

### 5. Profit Analysis
- Evaluated profitability across categories
- Compared revenue vs profit trends

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest sales
- Sales show variation over time with noticeable peaks
- A few products contribute significantly to overall revenue
- Certain regions outperform others consistently
- Profitability differs across categories, indicating cost variations

---

## 💡 Conclusion
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance. Insights from this analysis can help optimize product strategy, improve regional focus, and enhance profitability.
